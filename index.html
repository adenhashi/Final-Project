Let Me Introduce Myself

My name is Aden Hashi. I am a front-end web developer with experience in graphic design. I graduated from Central Washington University. My passion for coding ignited when I saw how coding brings in unlimited possibilities to create and execute what I design, so I taught myself HTML and CSS on my own time.

Deciding to pursue a career in front-end web development, I went to General Assembly to learn a wide range of coding, including Javascript and jQuery. My ultimate goal is to merge both of my front-end web development skills and my vast design techniques, contributing them to various industries as a technology that is constantly evolving and bringing in a high demand for coding.
